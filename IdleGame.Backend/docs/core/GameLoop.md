# Game Loop

The core gameplay loop is simple, readable, and designed for long‑term engagement. Generators produce Dust automatically over
time, and the player accumulates this Dust passively. Dust is then spent to unlock new generators and level up existing ones,
increasing total production and accelerating progression. As new generators unlock, the economy expands and the player gains
access to stronger sources of Dust generation.

The loop evolves across the game’s pacing arc:
    early levels progress quickly
    mid‑game upgrades become meaningful investments
    late‑game upgrades feel like major milestones

The exponential cost curve naturally creates a soft cap in the mid‑30s, shaping the long‑term rhythm of progression.
This loop repeats indefinitely and forms the foundation of the game’s early, mid, and late‑game experience.
